Who I Am

Welcome to my online portfolio. My name is Carl Lombardi and I am a full-stack developer, aiming to bring a fresh perspective to the industry. I began my career in the insurance field, working over nine years at American International Group in the Excess Casualty Claims department. After departing from AIG, I have decided to make a career change into web development.

When it comes to aesthetics, I tend to favor a clean, minimilist style with splashes of color where appropriate. I believe the best websites are easy to navigate, do more with less, and allow the end user to explore freely.

I am a 2009 graduate of Fairfield University in Fairfield, CT, with a B.A. in English/Journalism. As of September 2019, I will have completed a Full Stack Bootcamp through Rutgers University.

During my time at Rutgers Bootcamp, I have gained front end experience with HTML, CSS, Javascript and React. I have experience using Node.JS, MongoDB and MySQL databases for server side development as well.
